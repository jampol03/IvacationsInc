Philippines to Tanzania Adventure Package

Embark on a breathtaking journey to Tanzania. From the iconic Serengeti National Park to the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro and the pristine beaches of Zanzibar, this package guarantees an unforgettable adventure.

What's Included:

  • Flight Booking: Round-trip airfare from Manila to Kilimanjaro or Zanzibar.
  • Hotel Accommodation: 7 nights in 4-star lodges, safari camps, or beach resorts in Tanzania.
  • Tours: Guided safaris in Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater, trekking tours to Mount Kilimanjaro, and beach relaxation in Zanzibar.
  • Transportation: Private transfers and domestic flights for seamless travel.
  • Visa Assistance: Hassle-free visa processing for your trip to Tanzania.
  • Travel Insurance: Comprehensive travel insurance for peace of mind.
  • Dining Experience: Traditional Tanzanian meals, including nyama choma and pilau rice.

Philippines to Kenya Adventure Package

Experience the breathtaking wildlife and stunning landscapes of Kenya. From the world-famous Maasai Mara to the scenic Great Rift Valley, this package is perfect for adventure lovers and nature enthusiasts.

What's Included:

  • Flight Booking: Round-trip airfare from Manila to Nairobi.
  • Hotel Accommodation: 7 nights in 4-star lodges and hotels in Nairobi, Maasai Mara, or Amboseli.
  • Tours: Guided safaris in Maasai Mara, Lake Nakuru, and Amboseli National Parks.
  • Transportation: Private 4x4 safari vehicles for game drives and transfers.
  • Visa Assistance: Hassle-free visa processing for your trip to Kenya.
  • Travel Insurance: Comprehensive travel insurance for peace of mind.
  • Dining Experience: Traditional Kenyan meals, including nyama choma and ugali.

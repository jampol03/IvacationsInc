Philippines to Korea Adventure Package

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and history of Korea with our exclusive package. From ancient palaces to picturesque natural landscapes, Korea is home to some of the most iconic tourist attractions in Asia. This tour offers a perfect balance of tradition, beauty, and modernity.

What's Included:

  • Flight Booking: Round-trip airfare from Manila to Seoul.
  • Hotel Accommodation: 5 nights at a 4-star hotel in Seoul or nearby scenic locations.
  • Tours: Guided tours to Gyeongbokgung Palace, Nami Island, and Seoraksan National Park.
  • Transportation: Private airport transfers and comfortable tour buses.
  • Visa Assistance: Hassle-free Korea visa processing for your trip.
  • Travel Insurance: Comprehensive travel insurance for peace of mind.
  • Food Experience: Traditional Korean meals, including bibimbap and Korean BBQ tastings.

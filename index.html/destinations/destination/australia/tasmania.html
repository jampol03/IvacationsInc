Philippines to Tasmania Adventure Package

Explore the unspoiled beauty of Tasmania, Australia’s island state. Known for its rugged wilderness, pristine beaches, and unique wildlife, Tasmania offers an unforgettable adventure.

  • Dining Experience: Tasmanian seafood, cheese, and wine tastings.

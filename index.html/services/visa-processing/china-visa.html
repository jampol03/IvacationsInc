China Visa Requirements

Below is a detailed checklist of documents required for a China visa application. Please ensure all documents are complete before submission:

General Requirements

  • Completely filled and signed visa application form generated from the online portal of China Embassy.
  • Affixed with two (2) passport size (48mm x 33mm) colored photos with white background.
  • Original passport valid for at least another 6 months with at least one blank visa page.
  • Daily itinerary - schedule of daily activities with hotel address and contact number.
  • Ticket and hotel accommodation - photocopy of round-trip airline ticket and hotel reservation.
  • Bank certificate containing account type, account number, opening date, and balance (minimum PHP100,000).
  • Photocopy of latest Income Tax Return (ITR) with BIR received stamp.

First-Time Applicants

  • For Employed:
    • Certificate of Employment detailing salary, position, office address, and HR contact information.
    • Copy of company ID.
  • For Businessmen:
    • DTI or SEC Certificate - photocopy of business registration.
    • Business Permit or Mayor's Permit - photocopy of the permit.
    • Income Tax Return with receipt from BIR - photocopy.
  • For Professionals:
    • PRC ID (photocopy front and back).
  • For Students/Minors:
    • Original school certificate issued by the registrar.
    • School ID (clear photocopy).
    • Birth certificate (original issued by PSA).
    • Marriage contract of parents (original PSA).
    • Affidavit of support (notarized) and financial documents of parents.

Previous China Visa Applicants

  • Completely filled and signed visa application form generated from the online portal of China Embassy.
  • Original passport with blank pages and at least six (6) months validity left before expiration.
  • Affixed two (2) passport size (48mm x 33mm) colored photos with white background.
  • Attached previously used China visa in old or current passport (only visa stickers will be accepted).
  • Daily itinerary - schedule of daily activities including hotel address and contact number.
  • Hotel booking confirmation or hotel voucher.
  • A copy of the round-trip plane ticket.

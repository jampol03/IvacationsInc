Korean Visa Requirements

Below is a detailed checklist of documents required for a Korean visa application. Please ensure all documents are complete before submission:

Visa Requirements for Employees

  • Application Form (Must Be Computerized, Printed in A4, Font: Arial, Size:12, No Blanks, N/A if Not Applicable)
  • 1 Passport Size Colored Picture (3.5cm x 4.5cm, recent picture with white background to be glued (not stapled) on application form)
  • Original Passport (6 Months Valid) & Old Passport if with Travel History for the Past 5 Years
  • Copy of Passport First Page (Bio-Data Page)
  • Original & Copy of Valid Visa and Arrival Stamps to OECD Member Countries for the Past 5 Years
  • Original Employment Certificate (Must Include Applicant’s Position, Date Hired, Compensation, Address, Office Landline No. (Cell-Phone Number Is Not Allowed), E-Mail Address)
  • Copy of PRC Card/IBP Card
  • If No PRC or IBP Card:
    • Original Personal Bank Certificate (Must Include Account Type, Current Balance, Account Opening Date, 6 Months Average Balance (ADB))
    • Bank Statement (Original or Certified True Copy of Bank Statements/Passbook for the Last 3 Months)
    • Photocopy of ITR (Income Tax Return) or Form 2316

Visa Requirements for Businessmen

  • Application Form (Must Be Computerized, Printed in A4, Font: Arial, Size:12, No Blanks, N/A if Not Applicable)
  • 1 Passport Size Colored Picture (3.5cm x 4.5cm, recent picture with white background to be glued (not stapled) on application form)
  • Original Passport (6 Months Valid) & Old Passport if with Travel History for the Past 5 Years
  • Copy of Passport First Page (Bio-Data Page)
  • Original & Copy of Valid Visa and Arrival Stamps to OECD Member Countries for the Past 5 Years
  • Business Registration from SEC (Including GIS) or DTI (Copy)
  • Updated Business Permit or Mayor’s Permit (Copy)
  • Copy of PRC Card/IBP Card
  • If No PRC or IBP Card:
    • Original Personal Bank Certificate (Must Include Account Type, Current Balance, Account Opening Date, 6 Months Average Balance (ADB))
    • Bank Statement (Original or Certified True Copy of Bank Statements/Passbook for the Last 3 Months)
    • Photocopy of ITR (Income Tax Return) or Form 2316

Visa Requirements for Retire/Senior Citizen

  • Application Form (Must Be Computerized, Printed in A4, Font: Arial, Size:12, No Blanks, N/A if Not Applicable)
  • 1 Passport Size Colored Picture (3.5cm x 4.5cm, recent picture with white background to be glued (not stapled) on application form)
  • Original Passport (6 Months Valid) & Old Passport if with Travel History for the Past 5 Years
  • Copy of Passport First Page (Bio-Data Page)
  • Original & Copy of Valid Visa and Arrival Stamps to OECD Member Countries for the Past 5 Years
  • Senior Citizen ID (Copy)
  • Original Certificate of Retirement (If Applicable)
  • Original Personal Bank Certificate (Must Include Account Type, Current Balance, Account Opening Date, 6 Months Average Balance (ADB))
  • Bank Statement (Original or Certified True Copy of Bank Statements/Passbook for the Last 3 Months)
  • Applicants May Submit Other Proof of Funds (Land Titles, Vehicle Registration, Pension Certificate, Country Club or Golf Club, Stocks Certificate as Supporting Documents in Lieu of Required Documents)

Visa Requirements for Housewife / Househusband / Unemployed

  • Application Form (Must Be Computerized, Printed in A4, Font: Arial, Size:12, No Blanks, N/A if Not Applicable)
  • 1 Passport Size Colored Picture (3.5cm x 4.5cm, recent picture with white background to be glued (not stapled) on application form)
  • Original Passport (6 Months Valid) & Old Passport if with Travel History for the Past 5 Years
  • Copy of Passport First Page (Bio-Data Page)
  • Original & Copy of Valid Visa and Arrival Stamps to OECD Member Countries for the Past 5 Years
  • Sponsor’s Documents:
  • If Employed:
    • Employment Certificate (Original)
    • Original Personal Bank Certificate
    • Bank Statement (Original or Certified True Copy of Bank Statements/Passbook for the Last 3 Months)
    • Photocopy of ITR (Income Tax Return) or Form 2316
    • PSA Marriage Certificate (Original)
  • If Self-Employed:
    • Business Registration from SEC (Including GIS) or DTI (Copy)
    • Updated Business Permit or Mayor’s Permit (Copy)
    • Original Personal Bank Certificate
    • Bank Statement (Original or Certified True Copy of Bank Statements/Passbook for the Last 3 Months)
    • Photocopy of ITR (Income Tax Return) or Form 1701
    • PSA Marriage Certificate (Original)

Japan Visa Requirements

Below are the requirements for applying for a Japan visa. Please ensure all documents are complete before submission.

General Requirements:

  • Passport (Holder’s signature required) - Submit passport with issued old Japan visa if applicable.
  • Application Form (Printed on A4 size paper - STRICTLY type-written signed with black ink).
  • Photo (4.5×3.5cm).
  • PSA issued Birth Certificate and Marriage Certificate (for married applicants), issued within 1 year.
  • Itinerary in Japan (Printed on A4 size paper).
  • Multiple Entry Request Form (optional).
  • Pax Authorization Form (Printed on A4 size paper, 2 copies with wet-signature).
  • PSA Authorization Form (Printed on A4 size paper).
  • Waiver.

Additional Requirements:

  • If Birth Certificate is “LATE REGISTRATION,” submit Baptismal Certificate and School Record (Form 137).
  • If there is no record of Birth/Marriage in PSA, submit Birth Certificate issued by Local Civil Registrar and Negative Certificate issued by PSA.
  • Affidavit of discrepancy - for incomplete details or wrong information.

For Applicants Shouldering Travel Expenses:

  • Applicant’s Bank Certificate (balance within the last six months must be shown).
  • Applicant’s Tax Payment Certificate (Form 2316, signed by Employer and Employee).
  • Applicant’s Employment Certificate (COE) - must indicate period of employment, salary, and position.
  • For business owners, submit DTI Certificate of Business Name Registration or SEC and Mayor’s Permit from the City Hall.
  • For students, submit a copy of School ID or Certificate of Enrollment in School.
  • For government employees, submit a copy of approved leave of absence.
  • For seniors, submit a copy of Senior Citizen ID.

For Guarantors in the Philippines:

  • Guarantee Letter (Printed on A4 size paper).
  • Proof of relationship between applicant and guarantor (e.g., Birth Certificates or Photos).
  • Bank Certificate.
  • Photocopy of Tax Payment Certificate.
  • Employment Certificate OR Business Documents.

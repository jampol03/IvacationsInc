Philippines Tour

Discover the beauty of the Philippines with our exclusive travel packages. Explore Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, each offering unique experiences and breathtaking destinations.

Top Destination

Boracay

Famous for its white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters, Boracay is a tropical paradise that offers a perfect escape.

Luzon

Mountains of Luzon

Experience the stunning mountain ranges, historical landmarks, and vibrant culture of Luzon.

Vigan City

Walk through the historic streets of Vigan, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its preserved Spanish colonial architecture.

Mayon Volcano

Marvel at the perfect cone-shaped Mayon Volcano in Albay, a must-see natural attraction in Luzon.

Banaue Rice Terraces

Admire the breathtaking Banaue Rice Terraces, a 2,000-year-old engineering marvel carved into the mountains of Ifugao.

Pagudpud

Relax in Pagudpud, a serene coastal paradise in Ilocos Norte known for its pristine beaches and windmills.

Visayas

Chocolate Hills

Visit Bohol to see the unique geological formations of the Chocolate Hills, a natural wonder of the Philippines.

Kawasan Falls

Swim in the turquoise waters of Kawasan Falls in Cebu, a popular spot for canyoneering and nature lovers.

Malapascua Island

Explore the diving paradise of Malapascua Island, renowned for its thresher shark dives and pristine beaches.

Siquijor Island

Visit the mystical Siquijor Island, known for its waterfalls, caves, and folk healing traditions.

Antique's Kawa Hot Bath

Relax in a traditional kawa hot bath in Antique, nestled amidst lush greenery and scenic landscapes.

Mindanao

Samal Island

Relax on the pristine beaches of Samal Island, known for its luxurious resorts and vibrant marine life.

Lake Sebu

Immerse yourself in the cultural heritage and stunning landscapes of Lake Sebu, the ancestral domain of the T'boli people.

Mount Apo

Conquer Mount Apo, the highest peak in the Philippines, and enjoy its majestic views and biodiversity.

Hinatuan Enchanted River

Discover the mystical Hinatuan Enchanted River, a deep spring river with crystal-clear blue waters.

Tinuy-an Falls

Visit Tinuy-an Falls in Surigao del Sur, often referred to as the "Niagara Falls of the Philippines."

Palawan

Puerto Princesa Underground River

Explore the Puerto Princesa Underground River, one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature, located in the heart of Palawan.

Coron

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Coron, famed for its shipwreck diving spots, lagoons, and stunning coral reefs.

El Nido

Discover the breathtaking beauty of El Nido, known for its hidden lagoons, towering karst cliffs, and pristine beaches.

Tubbataha Reefs

Visit the Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a premier diving destination.

Balabac Islands

Unwind in the secluded Balabac Islands, known for its untouched beaches, turquoise waters, and rich marine life.

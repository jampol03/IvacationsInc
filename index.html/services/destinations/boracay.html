Unforgettable Boracay Trip

Experience the beauty of Boracay, a tropical paradise famous for its powdery white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife. Book your dream vacation now!

What's Included:

  • Flight Booking: Round-trip airfare from Manila to Caticlan or Kalibo.
  • Hotel Accommodation: 4 days and 3 nights at a beachfront resort.
  • Island Hopping Tour: Explore nearby islands with a guided boat tour.
  • Water Activities: Parasailing, kayaking, snorkeling, and paddleboarding.
  • Sunset Cruise: Enjoy a relaxing cruise with breathtaking sunset views.
  • Restaurant Discounts: Exclusive discounts at top Boracay restaurants.
  • Transportation: Seamless transfers from the airport to your hotel.

Top Activities in Boracay:

  • White Beach: Relax on Boracay's most famous beach, known for its pristine white sand and turquoise waters.
  • Willy's Rock: Visit this iconic landmark and take memorable photos against the stunning backdrop of the sea.
  • Bulabog Beach: Perfect for adrenaline junkies with activities like kiteboarding and windsurfing.
  • D'Mall: Shop for souvenirs, dine at local restaurants, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of this shopping hub.
  • Mount Luho: Hike to the island's highest point for panoramic views of Boracay's beauty.

Conquer the Heights of Mount Apo

Mount Apo, the highest peak in the Philippines, is a dream destination for trekkers and nature lovers. Located in Mindanao, this majestic mountain offers breathtaking views, diverse ecosystems, and a challenging yet rewarding adventure.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Davao City or Kidapawan to the Mount Apo trailhead.
  • Guided Trek: Experienced guides to lead you safely through the trails.
  • Camping Gear: Tents, sleeping bags, and cooking equipment provided.
  • Meals: Full-board meals during the trek.
  • Environmental Fees: Includes all necessary permits and conservation fees.
  • Summit Certificate: A token to commemorate your achievement of reaching the summit.

Top Activities at Mount Apo:

  • Trekking: Challenge yourself with a multi-day trek to the summit of Mount Apo.
  • Camping: Spend nights under the stars surrounded by the beauty of nature.
  • Wildlife Spotting: Encounter unique flora and fauna, including the Philippine eagle.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views from the summit and along the trails.
  • Hot Springs: Relax and rejuvenate in natural hot springs after your trek.

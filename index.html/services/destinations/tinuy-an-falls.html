Experience the Majestic Tinuy-an Falls

Tinuy-an Falls, located in Bislig City, Surigao del Sur, is often referred to as the "Niagara Falls of the Philippines." This multi-tiered waterfall is a breathtaking natural wonder and a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventurers.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Davao City to Tinuy-an Falls.
  • Guided Tour: Explore the falls and its surrounding natural beauty with a knowledgeable guide.
  • Entrance Fees: All environmental and entrance fees included.
  • Bamboo Raft Ride: Enjoy a unique experience by riding a bamboo raft beneath the cascading falls.
  • Meals: Packed lunch and snacks provided during the tour.
  • Photo Opportunities: Capture Instagram-worthy moments with the stunning backdrop of Tinuy-an Falls.

Top Activities at Tinuy-an Falls:

  • Bamboo Rafting: Float beneath the falls and feel the refreshing mist on your face.
  • Swimming: Take a dip in the crystal-clear pools at the base of the falls.
  • Hiking: Explore the surrounding forest and climb to the upper tiers of the waterfall.
  • Photography: Capture the majestic beauty of the multi-tiered cascades.
  • Picnicking: Enjoy a relaxing picnic with family and friends in the scenic area.

Discover the Mystical Charm of Siquijor Island

Siquijor Island, often referred to as the "Island of Fire," is a captivating destination known for its mystical traditions, pristine beaches, enchanting waterfalls, and friendly locals. A perfect getaway for relaxation and adventure seekers alike.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip ferry transfers from Dumaguete City to Siquijor Island.
  • Accommodation: 3 days and 2 nights at a beachfront resort.
  • Island Tour: Visit famous attractions like Cambugahay Falls, Salagdoong Beach, and Lazi Convent.
  • Meals: Daily breakfast and one traditional local dinner.
  • Snorkeling Gear: Complimentary use for exploring marine sanctuaries.
  • Local Guide: Friendly guides to share the island's history and folklore.

Top Activities in Siquijor Island:

  • Waterfall Adventures: Swim in the turquoise pools of Cambugahay Falls or take a refreshing swing from the vines.
  • Beach Hopping: Relax on the white sandy beaches of Salagdoong, Paliton, and more.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Explore vibrant coral reefs and marine sanctuaries around the island.
  • Visit Lazi Church and Convent: Marvel at the historic architecture of these Spanish-era landmarks.

Discover the Tropical Bliss of Samal Island

Samal Island, also known as the Island Garden City of Samal, is a tropical paradise located in Davao del Norte. Known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, Samal Island is a must-visit destination for relaxation and adventure.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip ferry transfers from Davao City to Samal Island.
  • Accommodation: 3 days and 2 nights at a beachfront resort.
  • Island Hopping: Explore nearby islands and hidden beaches.
  • Snorkeling Gear: Complimentary use for exploring vibrant coral reefs.
  • Meals: Daily breakfast and one seafood barbecue dinner.
  • Local Guide: Assistance during tours and activities.

Top Activities in Samal Island:

  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the white sandy beaches of Pearl Farm Beach Resort and other idyllic spots.
  • Island Hopping: Visit nearby islands such as Talikud Island and enjoy their pristine beauty.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Explore the vibrant underwater world, teeming with marine life.
  • Hagimit Falls: Take a refreshing dip in the cool waters of this scenic waterfall.

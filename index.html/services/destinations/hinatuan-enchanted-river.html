Discover the Magic of Hinatuan Enchanted River

Dive into the mesmerizing beauty of the Hinatuan Enchanted River, a mystical river in Surigao del Sur known for its deep blue waters and enchanting underwater scenery. Experience tranquility and wonder in this natural paradise.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Butuan City or Davao City to Hinatuan.
  • Entrance Fee: Access to the Enchanted River park and facilities.
  • Guided Tour: Learn about the history and legends surrounding the river.
  • Swimming Access: Enjoy swimming in designated areas of the river.
  • Picnic Area: Use of picnic huts for relaxation and meals.
  • Lunch: Fresh seafood lunch prepared by local cooks.

Top Activities at Hinatuan Enchanted River:

  • Swimming: Take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear blue waters.
  • Fish Feeding: Watch the daily fish feeding ritual where multicolored fish appear from the depths.
  • River Cruising: Enjoy a boat ride along the river to discover hidden lagoons and mangroves.
  • Photography: Capture the stunning beauty of the river and its surroundings.
  • Relaxation: Unwind in the serene environment, surrounded by lush greenery.

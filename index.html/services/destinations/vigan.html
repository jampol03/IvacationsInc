Step Back in Time at Vigan City

Vigan, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a charming city in Ilocos Sur known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture and cobblestone streets. Experience history, culture, and tradition in this fascinating destination.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Laoag or Manila to Vigan.
  • Accommodation: 3 days and 2 nights at a heritage hotel.
  • Guided Tour: Visit Calle Crisologo, Bantay Bell Tower, and other iconic landmarks.
  • Cultural Experiences: Learn pottery-making at Pagburnayan and explore the Vigan Heritage Village.
  • Meals: Daily breakfast and one Ilocano dinner featuring local delicacies such as bagnet and empanada.
  • Souvenir Stop: Shop for unique handcrafted items and local products.

Top Activities in Vigan:

  • Explore Calle Crisologo: Stroll through the iconic cobblestone street lined with ancestral houses.
  • Visit Bantay Bell Tower: Climb the historic bell tower for panoramic views of the city.
  • Learn Pottery: Try your hand at pottery-making at the Pagburnayan Jar Factory.
  • Heritage Tour: Discover the history and culture of Vigan through its museums and landmarks.
  • Food Trip: Savor Ilocano delicacies like Vigan longganisa, bagnet, and empanada.

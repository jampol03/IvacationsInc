Top Activities in Coron:

  • Kayangan Lake: Visit the cleanest lake in the Philippines, surrounded by limestone cliffs.
  • Twin Lagoon: Swim through crystal-clear waters and enjoy the serene beauty of this hidden gem.
  • World War II Wreck Diving: Explore historic shipwrecks that have become vibrant underwater ecosystems.
  • Maquinit Hot Springs: Relax and unwind in natural hot springs after a day of exploration.
  • Mt. Tapyas: Hike to the summit for panoramic views of Coron and its surrounding islands.

Dive into the Wonders of Tubbataha Reefs

Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a marine sanctuary located in the Sulu Sea. Known as one of the world's best diving destinations, it boasts pristine coral reefs, diverse marine species, and unparalleled natural beauty.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Puerto Princesa to the Tubbataha dive site via liveaboard vessel.
  • Liveaboard Accommodation: 5 days and 4 nights onboard a luxury dive boat.
  • Diving Equipment: Full gear rental for the duration of the trip.
  • Guided Dives: Multiple daily dives with experienced dive masters.
  • Meals: Full-board meals and snacks onboard.
  • Conservation Fees: Includes Tubbataha park fee and permits.

Top Activities at Tubbataha Reefs:

  • Scuba Diving: Explore vibrant coral gardens, walls, and drop-offs teeming with marine life.
  • Marine Wildlife Encounters: Spot reef sharks, manta rays, sea turtles, and a variety of fish species.
  • Photography: Capture the stunning underwater seascapes and thriving marine biodiversity.
  • Snorkeling: Enjoy shallow reef snorkeling for non-divers (availability depends on the liveaboard operator).
  • Relaxation: Unwind on the deck of the liveaboard while surrounded by the vast Sulu Sea.

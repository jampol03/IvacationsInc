Explore the Hidden Paradise of Balabac Islands

Discover the untouched beauty of the Balabac Islands in Palawan, a breathtaking destination with pristine beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. Perfect for those seeking a tranquil escape and unspoiled natural wonders.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip boat transfers to Balabac Islands.
  • Accommodation: 3 days and 2 nights in eco-friendly beach cottages.
  • Island Hopping: Guided tours to pristine islands such as Onuk Island, Candaraman Island, and Punta Sebaring.
  • Snorkeling: Explore vibrant coral reefs and marine sanctuaries.
  • Fresh Seafood: Enjoy meals prepared with the freshest catch of the day.
  • Local Guide: Learn about the culture and history of Balabac from experienced guides.

Top Activities in Balabac Islands:

  • Visit Onuk Island: Marvel at the powdery white sands and crystal-clear waters of this stunning private island.
  • Snorkeling: Dive into the waters of Candaraman Reef to see colorful corals and diverse marine life.
  • Punta Sebaring: Explore the softest sand you'll ever walk on, surrounded by lush mangroves and serene waters.
  • Star Gazing: Experience the beauty of the night sky, far from the lights of the city.
  • Wildlife Spotting: Catch a glimpse of rare species like the Philippine mouse-deer and monitor lizards.

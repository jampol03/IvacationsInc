Discover the Beauty of Malapascua Island

Malapascua Island, located off the northern coast of Cebu, is a tropical haven renowned for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and world-class diving spots. It's the perfect destination for divers and beach lovers alike.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Cebu City to Malapascua Island.
  • Accommodation: 3 days and 2 nights at a beachfront resort.
  • Diving Experience: Dive with thresher sharks and explore vibrant coral reefs.
  • Island Hopping: Visit nearby islands and sandbars, including Kalanggaman Island.
  • Snorkeling Gear: Complimentary use during tours.
  • Meals: Daily breakfast and one seafood barbecue dinner.

Top Activities in Malapascua:

  • Thresher Shark Diving: Malapascua is one of the few places in the world where you can dive with thresher sharks.
  • Snorkeling: Explore the vibrant underwater world, teeming with marine life.
  • Island Hopping: Visit nearby islands like Kalanggaman Island, known for its pristine sandbar.

Relax in the Paradise of Pagudpud

Pagudpud, located in Ilocos Norte, is a serene coastal town known for its white sandy beaches, turquoise waters, and breathtaking landscapes. Often called the "Boracay of the North," Pagudpud is a perfect destination for beach lovers and adventure seekers.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers from Laoag City to Pagudpud.
  • Accommodation: 3 days and 2 nights at a beachfront resort.
  • Beach Activities: Access to Blue Lagoon and Saud Beach.
  • Guided Tour: Visit famous landmarks such as Bangui Windmills, Kapurpurawan Rock Formation, and Patapat Viaduct.
  • Meals: Daily breakfast and one seafood feast dinner.
  • Local Guide: Friendly guides to ensure a rich cultural experience.

Top Activities in Pagudpud:

  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the pristine beaches of Blue Lagoon and Saud Beach.
  • Windmill Tour: Visit the iconic Bangui Windmills, the first power-generating wind farm in Southeast Asia.
  • Rock Formations: Marvel at the stunning Kapurpurawan Rock Formation.
  • Scenic Drives: Drive through the Patapat Viaduct, a coastal bridge offering spectacular views.
  • Water Sports: Enjoy activities such as surfing, snorkeling, or kayaking in the clear waters.

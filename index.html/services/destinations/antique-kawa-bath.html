Relax with Antique's Kawa Hot Bath

Discover the unique and rejuvenating experience of the Kawa Hot Bath in Antique. Immerse yourself in a giant cauldron filled with warm water, herbs, and flowers, surrounded by nature's tranquility.

What's Included:

  • Transportation: Round-trip transfers to the Kawa Bath site.
  • Kawa Hot Bath Session: Personalized herbal bath experience in a traditional kawa (cauldron).
  • Local Guide: Learn about the history and cultural significance of the Kawa Bath.
  • Refreshments: Complimentary herbal tea or fresh coconut juice.
  • Nature Walk: Enjoy a guided walk through lush greenery and scenic landscapes.

Top Activities in Antique:

  • Kawa Hot Bath: Relax in this unique outdoor spa experience that soothes your body and mind.
  • Bugang River: Experience river trekking or bamboo rafting along one of the cleanest rivers in the Philippines.
  • Seco Island: Visit this pristine sandbar, a hidden gem perfect for beach lovers and photographers.
  • Malumpati Cold Springs: Cool off in the crystal-clear waters of this natural spring.
  • Local Cuisine: Savor traditional Antique dishes like fresh seafood and native delicacies.

Top Activities at Puerto Princesa Underground River:

  • Underground River Tour: Marvel at the stalactites, stalagmites, and unique rock formations inside the cave.
  • Wildlife Spotting: Encounter monitor lizards, monkeys, and birds in the surrounding forests.
  • Photography: Capture the stunning karst landscapes and the serene river environment.
  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind at Sabang Beach before or after your tour.
  • Forest Trekking: Explore the lush jungle trails leading to the underground river.
